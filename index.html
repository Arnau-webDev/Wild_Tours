About Us

Who we are

We are a company specialised in running exotic exploring expeditions in full nature since 1992. We started out very little, by doing full guided mountain tours in our region, and due to our happy customers sharing their amazing experiences and our hard work, we have become what we are today. A top company in our sector that provides all kind of tours for all kind of clients all over the world.

What makes us so unique

We are a tour company like no other, we provide all kind of tours for all type of people. Beginning from full adventure wild survival safari, to a relaxed coast tour sleeping in cozy and luxurious hotels as well as exploring nature in its maximum expression. All that is possible with our services, and much more!
